About Under_Cover

We are Under_Cover, a team passionate about delivering fun, exciting, and challenging games for players of all ages. This project began as a group assignment but inspired us to keep building.

We originally considered a rock-paper-scissors game, but chose a sequence memory game instead — a true test of recall and quick thinking.
