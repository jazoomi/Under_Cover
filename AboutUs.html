About us:

We are Under_cover, a team that's passionate about delivering the most fun, exciting, and challenging games for the youth as well as adults.
This project stemmed from a group project as an assignment but seeing the progress of the page it has given inspiration to create more alike projects in the future! Originally we figured to do a rock-paper-scissors game, as it was too generic, we went with the other option of a sequence game. A game of test for memorization.
